Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Rifat Cholakov ("I", "me") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you visit rifatcholakov.com (the "Website"). I am committed to processing your data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable EU data protection law.

1. Data Controller

The data controller for this website is:
Rifat Cholakov — Europe
Email: contact@rifatcholakov.com

2. Data I Collect

  • Contact form data: Your name, email address, and message when you submit the contact form.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time on site, browser/device type — collected via Google Analytics (only if you consent).
  • Technical data: IP address, approximate location, and performance metrics — processed by Cloudflare as part of infrastructure security.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contact form submissions: Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — to respond to your inquiry.
  • Google Analytics: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — only activated if you accept analytics cookies.
  • Cloudflare: Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — to secure and deliver the website.
  • hCaptcha: Legitimate interest — to protect against automated spam submissions.

4.1 Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 to analyse traffic. It is only loaded if you provide explicit cookie consent. Data may be transferred to Google's servers in the US under standard contractual clauses.

Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Cloudflare

The website uses Cloudflare for performance and DDoS protection. Cloudflare may process your IP address and set strictly necessary cookies (__cf_bm, cf_clearance).

Privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

4.3 Web3Forms

Contact form submissions are processed by Web3Forms, which forwards them to my email. Data is not sold or used for marketing.

Privacy policy: web3forms.com/privacy

4.4 hCaptcha

The contact form uses hCaptcha for spam prevention. It may collect device and behavioural data for fraud detection.

Privacy policy: hcaptcha.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

  • Contact messages: Retained in email up to 2 years.
  • Google Analytics: Retained 14 months by Google.
  • Cloudflare logs: Retained up to 7 days.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU resident you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, or object to your data, and to withdraw consent for analytics at any time. Contact contact@rifatcholakov.com to exercise your rights. You may also lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.

See the Cookie Policy for a full list of cookies and how to manage them.

8. Changes

I may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top will reflect the latest version.
