Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how rifatcholakov.com uses cookies. Read alongside the Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website, allowing it to recognise your browser on future visits.

1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

Required for the website to function. No consent needed.

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
__cf_bm Cloudflare Bot management — distinguishes humans from automated traffic 30 minutes
cf_clearance Cloudflare Stores proof that a visitor passed a Cloudflare challenge 30 min – 1 day
cookie_consent This website Stores your cookie consent choice 1 year

2. Analytics Cookies

Only set after you accept analytics cookies via the consent banner.

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics Distinguishes unique users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics Stores and counts page views 2 years

hCaptcha

The contact form uses hCaptcha for bot protection. It may use temporary session values and device fingerprinting (strictly necessary security measure).

Managing Cookies

  • Click "Cookie Preferences" in the footer to update your consent
  • Use your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Use the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on
